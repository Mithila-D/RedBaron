About Team Red Baron

Team Red Baron is a group of undergraduate students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), Pune. Operating under PCCOE Motorsports, we design and develop an Electric All-Terrain Vehicle participating in eBaja SAE India. Previously designing and manufacturing combustion ATVs, we have participated in events such as Baja SAE India, Enduro Student India, Mega ATV Championship, and Baja SAE International. We design, manufacture, test, and race our ATV with only one aim in mind: Victory. We believe, ” We never lose. We either Win or Learn.”
